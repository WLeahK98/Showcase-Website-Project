The Goal

Reasons as to why I’d like to move into a career as a web developer, include but are not limited to the following, in no order of importance:

Reasons why I’d like to move into a career as a web developer

Reason 1

Problem-Solving and Creativity

I’ve always been fascinated by learning to code. I honestly thought it was pretty cool. I had previously tried building a basic website using Notepad, with the help of some YouTube tutorials, and thoroughly enjoyed it. The combination of creativity and technology—the fact that by writing lines of code you can not only influence the appearance of a webpage but also add effects and images—fascinates me.

Reason 2

Learning never ends

The fact that there are a myriad of different coding languages to choose from and learn also intrigues me. I also like the fact that theres always something new to discover in a field that is constantly evolving. As someone who is committed to continuous proffesional development, web development offers both challenging problems and the opportunity to implement innovative solutions,to learn, grow and excel in an exciting field.As well as the opportunity to use a wide range of technologies and learn how to write professional, high-quality code.All of these intriguing aspects, and more, helped solidify my decision to pursue a career as a software developer.

Reason 3

Potential for growth

I have noticed that the IT industry, including software, is in a constant perpetual state of growth and moving fast. It is an industry that is always changing and new technology is always being invented, and as someone who loves learning, creativity and actively seeks out challenges to develop new skills, that means there is never a dull moment and many opportunities for growth.

Reason 4

Teamwork and collaboration

I am also quite a social person and would love to get to know and meet new people and collaborate as a team on exciting projects along side like minded individuals who share a passion for creativity, technology and web development as I do.

Reason 5

Its enjoyable!

And last but not least, I find web development fun!
